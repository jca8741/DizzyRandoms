WELCOME!

“My mission in life: to reinvent the wheel by staying true to myself and to the values in life”. Capturing the nostalgic sounds of yesteryear while pushing the creativity and innovation of the future, Chicago born Noah Duncan aka Dizzy Randoms is here with a new twist on fashion and art. His creative vision derived from his discovery of music production as a seventeen year old boy.

From the summers of 2014 to 2017, Dizzy refined his production skills on the well known software, Ableton. His minimalistic lo-fi grooves and half timed beats came from a combination of the exploration of dub reggae from his father’s record collection and his mother’s new wave catalogue. Motivated by the rhythms of the music, Noah is working on launching a fashion brand as well as releasing visual art.
